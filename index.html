Glass-like distortions for your static page.

Scroll the page and watch the subtle chromatic aberration, glow, and coma ripple across the content as if viewed through imperfect cinema glass.

Intentional Imperfections

Cinematic lenses often introduce artifacts like coma, halation, and subtle bloom. Here, those aesthetics are recreated in real-time shaders while leaving the HTML untouched and fully scrollable.

Chromatic Aberration

Per-channel offsets on the edges of the viewport mimic the way light splits through glass.

Halation & Bloom

Bright highlights bleed softly, producing the dreamy glow of vintage cinema lenses.

Coma Distortion

Off-axis rays stretch and swirl, giving motion and depth even when the page is still.

Smooth Scrolling

Lenis keeps the scroll buttery so the shader can dance fluidly with your movement.

How it works

The DOM renders normally, then is sampled into a WebGL texture for post-processing. A full-screen quad applies fragment shaders that layer aberration, bloom, and noise without interfering with your layout or interactions.

Built for experimenting with three.js post-processing on everyday content.
